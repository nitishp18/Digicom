CCTV Solutions

We are Axis Certified integrators and provide complete CCTV system solutions from Analog, IP, Hybrid – Wired & Wireless. Our solutions integrate regular dome camera’s, Bullet camera’s, PTZ camera’s, Fish Eye - 360◦ , covert camera’s with Audio.

Our solutions enable users to view their systems from a variety of devices like monitors, PC’s, Tablets, Mobiles etc. The systems can be integrated with a variety of devices like Access Control, Motion sensors, Fire sensors etc..

We have special solutions for providing wireless CCTV solutions for high rise buildings & elevators.
