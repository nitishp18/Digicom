Digital Communication systems

We offer telecom solutions ranging from 8 lines to 2000 lines. Our solutions are built upon Universal Connectivity concept. It means, irrespective of the system configuration, the platform supports all current technologies like Analog, PRI, E&M, Wireless (VHF - UHF), GSM, CDMA, & VoIP.

The platform supports Analog, Digital, IP, Video, Mobile phones as endpoints. Solutions for integration CTI & CRM Interfaces, Public address, Intrusion sensors are easily deployable.

Dealing room solutions

We offer a very cost effective Digital solution for dealing rooms in form of Turrets. This system is capable of connecting to Analog Lines, PRI lines, Hotlines and Digital Turrets. The system also supports Hot Redundancy and call logging options. We have specially designed dual channel headsets for turrets with mute facility.As the system supports PBX features, it eliminates the need of having a separate PBX system in the premise.
